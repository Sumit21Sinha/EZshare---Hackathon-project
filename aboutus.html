Your History

EZshare is a web-based platform designed to facilitate sharing and borrowing of essential items among students living in hostels. The platform aims to promote a sense of community, reduce waste, and provide a convenient solution for students to access items they need without having to purchase them. Students can create a request for an item they need, specifying the duration and any additional requirements. Students who own the requested item can offer to lend it, specifying the rental period and cost. The platform will facilitate communication between the borrower and lender to negotiate the terms.

The few of the benefits of using this website are :

  • Convenience: Students can access essential items without having to purchase them.
  • Cost-Effective: Reduces waste and saves students money by promoting sharing and borrowing.
  • Community Building: Fosters a sense of community among hostel students, promoting social connections and collaboration.
  • Sustainability: Encourages sharing and reuse, reducing the environmental impact of unnecessary purchases.
